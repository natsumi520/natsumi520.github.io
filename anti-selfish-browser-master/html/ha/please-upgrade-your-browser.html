Lura: Ayyukan tallafi don Internet Explorer an dakatar da shi

Bayan watan Janairu 12, 2016, Microsoft ba ta samar da tallafi mai dacewa da sabuntawa don watau 11 da ƙasa ba.Ba tare da sabuntawar tsaro mai zurfi ba, kwamfutarka na iya zama mai rauni ga ƙwayoyin cuta, kayan leken asiri, da sauran software na cutarwa wanda zai iya sata ko lalata bayanan kasuwancinku da bayani.

Tun daga Nuwamba 30, 2020, sigar yanar gizo na kungiyar Microsoft ba za a iya samun dama ta amfani da Internet Explorer 11. Sauran aikace-aikacen Microsoft 30 a watan Agusta, 2021. Mai binciken da kanta za a ci gaba da tallafawa tsawon rayuwarWindows sigar da aka shigar har zuwa Yuni 15, 2022.Microsoft taiɗa Internet Yi ƙaura zuwa maɓallin "Internet Explorer" wanda ke ba da tallafi ga aikace-aikacen Intanet.

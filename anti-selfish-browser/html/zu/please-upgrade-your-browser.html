Usebenzisa inguqulo yakudala ye-Internet Explorer (IE11 noma ngaphansi noma isiphequluli usebenzisa le kernel).Lokhu kusho ukuthi ngeke ukwazi ukufinyelela kule webhusayithi uze uthuthukise isiphequluli sakho.

Uyacelwa uqaphele: Izinsizakalo zokusekela ze-Internet Explorer ziyekile

Ngemuva kukaJanuwari 12, 2016, iMicrosoft ayisahlinzeki ngokusekelwa okuhambisanayo kanye nezibuyekezo ze-IE 11 nangaphansi.Ngaphandle kokuvuselelwa kokuphepha kwesiphequluli esibucayi, ikhompyutha yakho ingaba sengozini yamagciwane ayingozi, i-spyware, nakwezinye i-software enonya engantshontsha noma ilimaze idatha yakho yebhizinisi nolwazi.

Kusukela ngoNovemba 30, 2020, inguqulo yeWebhu yamaqembu e-Microsoft ayisatholakali kusetshenziswa i-Internet Explorer 11. Izicelo ezisele zeMicrosoft 365 zizokwehlisa ukwesekwa kwe-IE11 ngo-Agasti 17, 2021. Isiphequluli uqobo sizoqhubeka ukusekelwa ngokuphila komsebenzi weI-Windows Version lapho ifakwe kuwo kuze kube ngoJuni 15, 2022.Microsoft incoma abasebenzisi be-Internet Explorer bathuthele basebenzise futhi basebenzise imodi eyakhelwe ngaphakathi "Internet Explorer Mode" enika amandla ukwesekwa kwezicelo ze-Internet zefa.
